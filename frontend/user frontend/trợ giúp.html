Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my financial reports? expand_more
You can export reports by navigating to the Analytics tab, selecting "Financials", and clicking the "Export" button in the top right corner. Formats available include PDF and CSV.
Can I integrate IoT soil sensors? expand_more
Yes, AgriPlanner supports various IoT devices. Go to Settings > Integrations to connect your compatible soil moisture and temperature sensors via API keys.
How do I update livestock vaccination records? expand_more
Navigate to the Livestock dashboard, select the specific animal or herd, and click on the "Medical History" tab. From there, you can add new vaccination entries.
How do I reset my account password? expand_more
If you are logged out, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. If you are logged in, go to Settings > Security and click "Change Password" to send a reset link to your email.
